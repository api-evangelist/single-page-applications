Today, the Chicago Tribune introduced its new Digital Members-only content section in partnership with NewsCred.The exclusive section on chicagotribune.com provides journalism from the Tribune’s own newsroom as well as curated news and editorial content from world-class publishers such as the Economist and Forbes.Through NewsCred’s syndication and curation technology, the Chicago Tribune’s registered Digital Members will now be able to access some of the most relevant business and finance news.The Tribune is leveraging the additional editorial content to incentivize users of chicagotribune.
